Training Package Terms

Personal Training packages are valid for one year. Any sessions not used in this time frame will be forfeited. Package price is billed via cash, check or credit card. Payment must be received before training begins. Appointments can be scheduled up to one month in advance only.

Semi-Private Training memberships at any level requires a 4-month commitment; you will be charged the monthly amount. At the time of your fourth payment, you may cancel the contract or re-up for another four months. If you wish to cancel your contract, you must contact Tailored Strength to do so, otherwise the charge will automatically occur each month. Once you have used all your monthly allotment of sessions, any additional classes you wish to attend must be purchased at the drop-in rate. Unused sessions expire at the end of each month.

I understand that all Personal and Semi-Private training rates are based on set timed sessions and should I arrive late, there is no guarantee I will receive the full session with my trainer. In return, if my Personal Trainer is late for a session, I will still receive the full session time or an additional free session.

I understand that Tailored Strength bills its Personal and Semi-Private Training clients on a pre-pay basis. Once my trainer and I have decided upon the type of training package and payment plan I will purchase, payment must be made before the sessions are conducted. I understand that all Personal and Semi-Private Training sessions are non-refundable.

Cancellations

Personal and Semi-Private Training clients are allowed 2 excused cancellations (due to illness, business, etc.) per package. To have a cancelled appointment considered excused, you must notify your Personal Trainer at least twenty-four (24) hours in advance of the scheduled workout. Each excused cancellation may be rescheduled for an alternate date and time within the same calendar month agreeable to both client and trainer. Tailored Strength recommends that all cancelled sessions be rescheduled to ensure consistency and fitness progress.

A canceled appointment will be considered unexcused when you do not show up for a scheduled training session or when you fail to meet the twenty-four (24) hour advanced notice condition. You will forfeit all unexcused cancellations. Tailored Strength recommends that all cancelled sessions be rescheduled to ensure consistency and fitness progress.

Refunds & Expiration

No refunds or credits for payments made or applied to Personal and Semi-Private Training packages will be issued. The sessions are nontransferable. If the trainer has to cancel due to illness or emergency, the session will be made up at the client's earliest convenience.
